ABOUT US

Organic spices refer to plant substances that are grown without pesticides, growth hormones, or lab-engineered genetic modification.

1.They are used for flavoring or coloring food and do not contain any volatile oil or other flavoring principle.

2.Oleoresins are a naturally occurring combination of oil and resin that can be extracted from plants. The extraction process begins with raw spices that are cleaned and ground, then the spices' oils are distilled using an organic solvent.

3.Organic, fair trade, and biodynamic spices include black and white pepper, cinnamon, cardamom, clove, nutmeg, mace, turmeric, and ginger.

4.The organic farms - if you have access of one in the neighborhood, it is very convenient to buy direct fresh produce from the farms.
